  • Gameplay: Two players will see a title screen with a start button. When one of the players presses the start button, a character select screen will open up with characters Ryu, Jotaro, Scorpion, and Goku (all of them play the same). Player 1 will pick one of the characters with the mouse. Once the player picks his/her character, he/she cannot pick a different character. Once the character is picked, the continue button must be clicked for player 2 to pick his/her character (he/she can only pick one as well). When the continue button is clicked again, the game will begin. Each player will fight each other to the death. Each player is also given life and super meters. Player 1 will start, and each player will rotate between turns.
    • Light Attack: Player deals 20 damage to the other player, but the other player will gain 20 points for his/her super meter.
    • Heavy Attack: Player deals 40 damage to the other player, but the other player will gain 40 points for his/her super meter.
    • Block Attack: Player will not receive damage not super meter points once this attack activates, but the other player who attacks will receive 20 super points for a light attack, or 40 super points for a heavy attack.
    • Super Attack: When a player's super meter is over 100 points, the super attack will appear for him/her. The Super Attack can deal 80 damage and 80 super points to the other player, even if that player used block attack.
    The player who's life meter is below 0 loses.
  • Strategies: The player will need use their attacks thoughtfully for it is a risk and award system. A player could only use heavy attacks at the risk of the other player gaining his super points faster. A player can use his block attack at the risk of the other player using his/her heavy attack to gain more points for his/her super meter. Strategy comes down solely on player's choice.

In four different worlds based off of famous video games and manga, four warriors have different reasons for fighting. Ryu wishes to become the strongest Street Fighter in order to control the rage from within. Jotaro uses his Stand Star Platinum to serve justice to evil doers. Scorpion only wishes slaughter other warriors until he finds his revenge against Sub-Zero. Finally, Goku only wants to fight against strong warriors to break his own limits. These iconic warriors are sent to our world by a mysterious force to fulfill their goals. Players get to determine would would win as they get to play matches created from their dreams.
